🤖 Agri Robotics in India

Smart Farming with AI, Drones & Automation

What is Agricultural Robotics?

Agricultural robotics uses drones, AI machines, and automation to improve productivity, reduce labor, and enable precision farming in modern agriculture.

Modern Technologies

🚁 Smart Drones

Used for spraying, monitoring crops and data collection.

🚜 Autonomous Tractors

Self-driving tractors improve efficiency and accuracy.

🌱 Harvesting Robots

Automatically detect and harvest ripe crops.
