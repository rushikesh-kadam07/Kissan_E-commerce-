Learn soil properties, water holding capacity, and suitable crops for better farming productivity.

🌾 Major Soil Types

Best crops according to soil condition

🖤 Black Soil

Black soil is rich in clay and minerals. It retains moisture for a long time and is ideal for cotton farming.

💧 Water Holding Capacity
High - 90%
🌱 Recommended Crops
Cotton Soybean Wheat Sunflower

❤️ Red Soil

Red soil contains iron and supports many crops. It has moderate water retention and good drainage.

💧 Water Holding Capacity
Medium - 60%
🌱 Recommended Crops
Groundnut Millets Potato Pulses

🟤 Sandy Soil

Sandy soil has larger particles and drains water quickly. Suitable for crops needing less water retention.

💧 Water Holding Capacity
Low - 35%
🌱 Recommended Crops
Watermelon Peanuts Carrot Cucumber

🌿 Smart Farming Tips

  • ✔️ Test soil before crop planting.
  • ✔️ Use organic fertilizers.
  • ✔️ Maintain irrigation properly.
  • ✔️ Use crop rotation methods.
  • ✔️ Protect soil moisture using mulching.
